# Math

## Timer

$$
f_{end}=\frac{f_{initial}}{PreScaler \cdot (f_{ARR})}\\
f_{ARR}=\frac 1 {ARR + 1}
$$

## ADC

$$
\begin{flalign}
&1 LSB = \frac{V_{REF}}{2^N}\\
&\text{Full Scale Range (FSR): } V_{FSR}=V_{REF}- LSB \\
&\text{Max Sample Rate: } f_{max}=\frac{f_{ADC}}{PreScaler}
\end{flalign}
$$
